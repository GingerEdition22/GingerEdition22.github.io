Okay, if you're here you're probably wondering who this guy is.

Well, that's SadBee. Yeah, it's "SadBee", not "sad bee" or "sad-bee" or anything. Don't ask why that is, cause I honestly don't know, that's just how I've always written it.

Yeah... But who is SadBee?

He's my little mascot, who has slowly evolved into my logo. Here's a brief history:

One day I was designing a bee to print out to decorate a door for a school project. I decided to make some happy bees, and some who seemed to be surpised by something. But I realized maybe I needed a sad bee to add some interest. Upon printing the bee my teacher (fairly so) didn't want to use it in the project. I ended up walking into my next period and upon sitting down another classmate noticed this sad bee still on my computer. He said that I should make it my profile picture. After some tweaking (making it 3D, adding a background) I ended up with the SadBee of today. Since then the file SadBee is in has evolved to boast over 70 artboards of bees with various emotions and outfits.

Below are some of the bees I've desgined. Many have been left out as the bees come on different backgrounds and with different levels of 3D effects.
